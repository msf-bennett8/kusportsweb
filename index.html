About Us

Welcome to Kenyatta University sports, your ultimate destination for everything in sports. We're more than just a website; we're a passionate community of sports enthusiasts, athletes, and fans. Our mission is to bring you closer to the world of sports, to inspire, educate, entertain, and to foster a sense of belonging among sports lovers worldwide.We are a group fostering to improve sport activity by majoring and focusing on technology and its application in sports.

Kenyatta University website was founded in August 2023 by founders Moitui Zablon and Mark Wandera, lifelong Modern Sports Practitoners and enthusiasts, Students at Kenyatta University an institution empowering change through technology. The idea behind this platform was simple,It came from our instructor Dr. Kiploto Boit that instructed us to create a space where sports enthusiasts could come together to celebrate their passion and find everything they need to stay updated and engaged with the sports they love as. Through constant consultation from him and research we were able to come up with this platform.
